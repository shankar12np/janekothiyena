Tax Deadlines

  • April 15th (or next business day): Deadline for filing federal income tax returns (Form 1040).
  • October 15th (with extension): Extended deadline for filing federal income tax returns if an extension was requested.
  • April 15th: 1st Quarter Estimated Tax Payment.
  • June 15th: 2nd Quarter Estimated Tax Payment.
  • September 15th: 3rd Quarter Estimated Tax Payment.
  • January 15th of the following year: 4th Quarter Estimated Tax Payment.
  • Personal property tax deadlines (October 5th) vary by state and locality. Check with your local tax authority for specific due dates related to personal property taxes, such as vehicle registration and business property taxes.
  • January 31st: Deadline for employers to provide W-2 forms to employees and 1099 forms to contractors.
  • March 15th (or the 15th day of the 3rd month after the end of the fiscal year for corporations): Deadline for filing corporate income tax returns (Form 1120 for C corporations).
  • April 1st: Deadline for certain small businesses to establish a SIMPLE IRA plan for the current year.
  • April 15th: Deadline for making contributions to traditional and Roth IRAs for the previous tax year.
  • May 17th (or the next business day if it falls on a weekend or holiday): Deadline for filing federal income tax returns and paying any taxes owed for the previous tax year in 2023.

    • Please note that these deadlines can change based on specific circumstances and tax law changes, so it's essential to verify the dates with the relevant tax authorities and consult a tax professional for guidance.
