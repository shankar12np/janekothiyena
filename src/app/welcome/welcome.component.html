Person Of The Month

Arjun Ranabhat

Biography: Arjun Ranabhat

A Source of Inspiration and Culinary Excellence

Early Life and Background:

Born in Pokhara, Nepal, Arjun embarked on his culinary journey after earning a Bachelor of Science in Hotel and Culinary Management from Vel's University in 2005, Chennai, India. Inspired by watching his mother in the kitchen, his love for cooking ignited early. Her delicious recipes and his desire to share his cultural dishes with Americans motivated him to become a chef. Growing up, the flavors of his mother's cooking sparked a passion for authentic and diverse cuisines. As Arjun pursued his career, he aimed to bring the rich tastes of his heritage to a broader audience. From his early days to becoming a Corporate Executive Chef, his journey is a flavorful tribute to his mother's culinary legacy and a way to introduce his nation's dishes to the American table.
